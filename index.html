Education & Training Highlights

Building Disaster Resilience Through Experiential Learning

A four-day intensive program equipping participants with climate science knowledge and response mechanisms through hands-on training.

  • Train-the-Trainer: Focused on empowering 27 diverse participants to champion resilience in their home communities.
  • Practical Modules: Included first-aid, CPR training by AIMS Kochi, and field visits to the Tsunami Memorial.

Resilient Futures: Navigating Climate Risks

An executive course for industry leaders focusing on practical tools for managing climate-related financial and operational risks.

  • Reporting Standards: Training on TCFD, ISSB, and BRSR frameworks.
  • Technical Analysis: Use of IPCC AR6 emission scenarios for corporate risk assessments.
